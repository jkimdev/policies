# Outpost - Privacy Policy
## English
Outpost is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection.

Data Collection and Usage: Outpost does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. However, if you use the Global Leaderboard feature, your game scores and nickname may be transmitted to our server to maintain the rankings. By using the leaderboard, you consent to the transmission of this specific data. All other game progress and settings are stored locally on your device.

Third-Party Services: Our app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services that collect your personal data.
