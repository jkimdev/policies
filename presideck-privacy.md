# Presideck - Privacy Policy

## English
Presideck is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection.

**Data Collection:** Presideck **does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information** or user data. All game progress and settings are stored locally on your device.

**Third-Party Services:** Our app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services that collect your data.
